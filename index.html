About Me

I'm Taylor, a self-taught web developer. I enjoy design, art, and practicality. Addicted to travel and observing cultures in motion, I've come to admire how humans have implemented design to facilitate day to day life. As culture continues to fill the internet, it's only natural that our tendencies for design and beauty follow. My journey of self-taught web development started with "I want to design something and put it out there for the world."

When not coding, I enjoy relaxing outside, reading, playing with my dogs Happy & Bo, learning languages (currently learning Mandarin Chinese), and making music.
